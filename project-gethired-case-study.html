GetHired AI
Case Study

An AI recruitment platform built to improve matching quality between candidates and recruiters using explainable scoring, CV intelligence, and role-specific automation workflows in English and French.

Background + Problem

Recruitment friction to solve

Candidate side

Applicants struggled to understand why they were rejected and how to improve. Generic feedback and static CV workflows made upskilling and interview readiness inefficient.

Recruiter side

  • High volume screening consumed too much time.
  • Ranking quality varied between reviewers.
  • Interview prep was manually repetitive.
  • Outreach targeting lacked consistent signals.
Technical Solution

System and intelligence architecture

Candidate Intelligence

CV parsing, semantic extraction, fit scoring, skill-gap detection, and personalized recommendations for certifications and interview preparation.

Recruiter Intelligence

Automated ranking, profile summarization, interview plan generation, and outreach support based on contextual scoring and role requirements.

Platform Stack

Next.js + TypeScript frontend, Spring Boot APIs, JWT auth, Groq LLM orchestration, and multilingual prompt/control flows.

Deliverables + Impact

What was delivered

Delivered modules

  • Candidate portal with CV enhancement assistant.
  • Recruiter dashboard with ranked candidate pools.
  • Interview planner and structured question generation.
  • Cross-language experience for EN/FR usage.

Outcome

The platform made screening workflows faster and more transparent while giving candidates actionable improvement paths instead of opaque outcomes.
